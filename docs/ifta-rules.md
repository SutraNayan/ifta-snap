# Georgia IFTA Tax Rates & Rules Reference

## 2026 Quarterly Rates (GA Dept. of Revenue)

| Quarter | Fuel | Rate (¢/gal) |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Q1 2026 (Jan–Mar) | Diesel | 37.3¢ |

## Filing Deadlines
- **Q1:** April 30
- **Q2:** July 31
- **Q3:** October 31
- **Q4:** January 31

## Late Payment Interest
- **Rate:** 9% annually (0.75% per month)
- **Authority:** GA Code Section 48-2-40

## Record Retention
- **Duration:** 4 years from filing date
- **Authority:** 2026 IFTA Procedures Manual
- **Format:** Digital scans acceptable as primary evidence

## Net Taxable Gallons Formula
```
Fleet MPG = Total Miles ÷ Total Diesel Gallons Purchased
Gallons Consumed in GA = GA Miles ÷ Fleet MPG
Net Taxable Gallons GA = Gallons Consumed GA − Gallons Purchased GA
Tax Due = Net Taxable Gallons GA × GA Rate
```

## Required Receipt Data Fields
Per IFTA Procedures Manual, each receipt must capture:
1. Seller Name (full legal name)
2. Seller Location — State and City
3. Fuel Type (Diesel/Gas/DEF)
4. Gallons (3 decimal places)
5. Date of purchase
6. Vehicle ID (unit number or license plate)
